excel 

Learning the basics of Excel has helped me understand how to organize and analyze data. I’ve learned to enter and format data in cells, create simple formulas like SUM and AVERAGE, and make charts to visualize information. I now know how to sort and filter data to find what I need quickly. I’ve also learned helpful shortcuts, such as **Ctrl + C** to copy, **Ctrl + V** to paste, and **Ctrl + Z** to undo mistakes. Using **Ctrl + Shift + L** for filtering and **Alt + =** for autosum has made working with Excel much faster and easier.
